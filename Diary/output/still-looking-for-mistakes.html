Still looking for mistakes

13 maggio 2016

Today I need to find out what is wrong with the inner controller vrft.

What I know:

  • The size of the error in the inner controller is data-dependent. With a step response it becomes incredibly big
  • The inner reference model compares very well to the actual system behaviour in simulink. They are the same (comparing \(q\) from ct system with \(y_i\)). The VRFT output is quite close but clearly not as good.
  • The ct and dt variants of InnerRefeenceModel have the same dynamics.

Considering that PlantModel_dt contains Mixer the presence of the unstable pole is worrying.

After another day of work I'm n closer to finding the solution to the problems. I created inner_vrft.mlx (mlx link) to re-do the vrft from scratch and came up with the same results. I have no idea where to go from here.
